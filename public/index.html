About Sree Vidyadhiraja Foundation

Recognized NGO, Govt. of India

Sree Vidyadhiraja Foundation is dedicated to promoting the values of Mahaguru Chattambi Swami. We organize charitable, educational, cultural, and spiritual programs to uplift society and preserve our heritage. Activities

Our Key Initiatives

Blood Donation

Organizing voluntary blood donation drives to save lives and support healthcare needs.

Free Medical Camps

Providing free health check-ups, medicines, and awareness programs for the underprivileged.

Treatment Assistance

Helping patients with financial support for critical treatments and hospital care.

Free Study Equipments

Distributing books, uniforms, and study materials to needy students for a brighter future.

Self Help Groups

Encouraging community-based groups for financial stability and mutual support.

Entrepreneurship Training

Providing skill-based training and guidance to encourage small-scale entrepreneurship.

Marketing Assistance

Helping small-scale entrepreneurs market and sell their products effectively.

Career Guidance Seminars

Conducting seminars and workshops to guide students in choosing their career paths.

Support for Home Construction

Extending financial and material support for the construction of homes for needy families.

Our Initiatives

Serving Society with Compassion

Food & Shelter Programs

We provide nutritious food, clean water, and safe shelter to families and individuals in need.

Support for Elderly & Orphans

We establish shelter homes, orphanages, and old age homes to ensure dignity and care for the vulnerable.

Educational & Social Welfare

Our programs focus on education, skill training, and community development for sustainable change.
